# Infactory API - Educational Demo Topic Research

**Date:** 2026-01-31
**API Base URL:** https://atlantichack-api.infactory.ai/v1/
**Search Method:** POST to `/search` with JSON body `{query, top_k, group_by}`

---

## Executive Summary

The Infactory API provides access to a rich corpus of Atlantic magazine articles spanning 2022-2025. After searching five educational topics, **"Artificial Intelligence"** and **"Climate Change"** emerged as the strongest candidates for an educational demo due to their rich coverage, varied time range, educational relevance, and compelling headlines.

## Recommendations

### Primary Recommendation: Artificial Intelligence

**Why it works for demo:**
1. **Timely and engaging** - AI is the topic of 2023-2025; immediate student interest
2. **Rich philosophical content** - "Of God and Machines" raises questions about consciousness and humanity
3. **Critical perspectives** - "AI Isn't Omnipotent. It's Janky." provides balance against hype
4. **Diverse angles** - Technology, ethics, art, society, democracy
5. **Compelling headlines** - Titles like "Welcome to the Big Blur" and "In Defense of Humanity" draw readers in

**Demo Potential:**
- Compare attitudes toward AI across years
- Explore the hype vs. reality narrative
- Discuss AI's impact on creativity, jobs, and democracy

## API Notes

- **Endpoint:** POST `/v1/search`
- **Authentication:** `X-API-Key` header
- **Response Format:** Results grouped by year with article metadata (title, author, section, topic, excerpt)
- **Topics endpoint** (`/v1/topics`) was unresponsive during testing (timeout)
- **Search is hybrid mode** with deduplication enabled
- **Scoring:** RRF (Reciprocal Rank Fusion) scores provided for result ranking
