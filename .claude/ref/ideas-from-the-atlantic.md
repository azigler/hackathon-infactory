**Here are some prompts from The Atlantic’s product team to spark your thinking:**

* How might we make The Atlantic accessible and useful to high school and/or college students? To high school teachers and college professors? 

* How might we responsibly use AI to help journalists across the industry research and source their stories? 

* How might we expand a user’s abilities to interact with The Atlantic archive in a multimodal capacity?

* How might we leverage personalization to help users discover content from The Atlantic they might not be aware of? 

* How might we leverage The Atlantic’s archive to enrich and add context to on-platform user conversations and discussion around today’s issues?

* What might a publisher-forward LLM experience look like? It should prioritize clear credit and linking back to the publisher, plus a pathway to audience or revenue growth.
